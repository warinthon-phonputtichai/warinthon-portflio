WHAT IS BU HIDE AND SEEK

Hide and Seek is a game where players are divided into two teams: "hiders" and "seekers." The seekers must find the hiders and touch them to return to the starting point, while the hiders must try to avoid being found. The game has the following time and scoring rules: Start time: 30 seconds, Total game time: 300 seconds (5 minutes), Time allowed for hiders to hide: 80 seconds, Points awarded for hiders: 5 points, and Points awarded for seekers: 5 points.

GAME OPERATION

After the game starts, it requires at least 2 players to begin. If fewer than 2 players are present, the game cannot start.

During the game, players are divided into "hiders" and "seekers." Seekers are randomly chosen and have a WalkSpeed of 45, while hiders have a WalkSpeed of 25.

When the game ends, a message is displayed to the winners, awarding them 5 points according to the rules. The game then resets by calling setUpGame and gameLoop to return to the starting state.
