FOOD DELIVERY

Make UI Food Delivery Follow Scenario "Lisa is a second-year student at Bangkok University who loves exercising, staying healthy, socializing with friends, and keeping up with the latest technology. She wants to order lunch to eat at Jenny's house, so she opens her usual app to place the order. After selecting the items, Lisa reviews the details, including the address, payment information, food items, and prices, then confirms the order. She then selects an existing user profile that has her saved address and credit card details, completing the payment".

UI EXAMPLE

Login and Sing Up Page.

When you login completed you will see Restaurant Page.

Once a restaurant is selected, the following menu will appear.

Once you have selected your menu, you can select the quantity you want to order.

When you have finished selecting the food items you want to order, you will come to the payment page and there will be a record of the address and payment, which completes the food order.
