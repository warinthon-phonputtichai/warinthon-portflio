The concept of EQUALECHO

We want to make it easier and more convenient for left-handed people to buy products that are defaulted to left-handed people, so we created a website called EqualEhco.

Features of the EQUALECHO

Product display page for left-handed people

"It allows users to find left-handed products such as scissors, notebooks, and kitchen knives, all of which can be used with the left hand."

Easy to use for left-handed people and everyone in general.

"We designed it so that all left-handed people have a place to buy left-handed products and make the website easy to use, like general shopping websites such as Shopee, Amazon."

Categorizing products to make it easier to find the products you need.

" Categorize products to make finding products easier, with categories such as kitchen, clothing, kids items, and general."
