Software Tester Internship

At JTS & LOOK LOOK Application

Objective LOOK LOOK

During my internship at Jasmine Technology Solution (JTS), I participated in the development and testing of the LOOK LOOK application. The purpose of this project was to provide users with a smooth and user-friendly mobile experience, both in terms of functionality and ease of use. My main responsibilities included designing and writing Test Cases to verify that the system functioned correctly across multiple features, as well as performing Automated Testing to enhance testing efficiency and ensure the stability and accuracy of the application.

Tools

Postman

Postman is a powerful tool used for testing APIs in the LOOK LOOK application, sending requests, validating responses, and ensuring backend functionality works correctly.

Appium

Appium is an open-source automation tool for performing mobile app testing on Android and iOS platforms, allowing efficient and reliable functional testing for LOOK LOOK.

Playwright

Playwright is a modern framework for automated end-to-end testing of web applications, used to ensure LOOK LOOK works consistently across multiple browsers.

Google Sheet

Google Sheet is used to document and manage Test Cases for LOOK LOOK, allowing organized tracking of test steps, expected results, actual results, and status in a collaborative environment.

Jira Board

Jira Board is a project management tool used to track tasks, bugs, and feature development in the LOOK LOOK application. It helps organize work in sprints, assign responsibilities, monitor progress, and ensure timely completion of testing and development tasks.
